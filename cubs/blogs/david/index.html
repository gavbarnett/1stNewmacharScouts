Gandalf the Great

Words: David

Gandalf relaxing

This is Gandalf, named after the Gandalf-the-Grey / White from the Hobbit and Lord of the Rings. He is a great hunter. He caught a rabbit 🐇 last night and nearly got a seagull!

Cats sleep for 12-16 hours a day. Our one wakes up at night and wants to go hunting for mice, voles, birds, rabbits and sometimes also my dad!
