My Nintendo Switch

Words: Charlie

One of my favourite things to do on a rainy day is to play on my Nintendo switch.

Before Lockdown my friend Ben would come over to play SSBU [Super Smash Bros Ultimate].

We like it because we get to play a variety of characters from different games like Mario, Link and Kirby and fight with them.

My top games include ARMS, Super Mario Maker 2, Minecraft, Nickelodeon Kart Racers, Sonic Mania Plus and Super Smash Bros Ultimate.
