Our Lockdown Pot

Planning what to do after Lockdown

Mum had an idea that at the end of each week we would sit down and each write something we miss doing on a piece of paper and put them all in a pot.

Then after lockdown is over and we want to do somethig, we will pick one of the things we have missed doing out of the pot andf do it.

Some of the things already in our pot:

  • Go to Knockburn Loch
  • Go to trampoline park
  • Go swimming
  • Go to the beach...

Why don't you start a pot of activities to do after lockdown at your house!?
